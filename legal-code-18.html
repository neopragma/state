Consumer Protection

18. Consumer Protection.
This section of the legal code concerns consumer protection.

18.1. Definitions.

18.1.1. Consumer. Anyone who purchases goods or services.

18.2. Transparency In Pricing.

The price for any goods or services must be easily available for consumers to see. The price shown prior to the consumer's decision to purchase must be the full amount that will be due in exchange for the promised goods and/or services.
