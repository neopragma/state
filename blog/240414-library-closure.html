Library Closure

14 April 2024

Fox News has been criticized and ridiculed for recent reports of public libraries turning into havens for drug abuse and venues for public sex, drunkenness, and violence. Given the track record of Fox News as a mouthpiece for the most extreme neo-Fascist/Republican propaganda, we thought it prudent to check into the credibility of these claims.

On the surface, Fox News reporting about public libraries is difficult to believe. Has the neo-Fascist movement reached the stage that they can brazenly say anything at all, however outrageously false, without accountability? Perhaps not. The most damaging lies are often those that contain a grain of truth; and there is a grain of truth here.

We found a single case of a public library that had gone out of control, and had indeed become a place where unruly behavior occurred on a routine basis, resulting in over 1,800 calls to the police over a period of a few years. But we did not find a pattern of public libraries devolving into unsafe places, as the Fox News reporting might lead consumers of the news to believe.

One branch of the Contra Costa County Library in Antioch, California, had experienced an ongoing series of incidents that included sex in the bathroom, drunks hanging out in the library and harassing patrons, drug sales in the parking lot, a hit-and-run in the parking lot, a burning mattress left in the parking lot, and assorted minor incidents besides those more-serious ones. Eventually the problems got so bad the branch closed its doors.

The problem with the way Fox News has reported the matter is that they crafted the story to make it seem as if the entire public library system nationwide had spiraled into decay.
